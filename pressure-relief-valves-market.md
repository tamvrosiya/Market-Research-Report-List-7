<p><p>The pressure relief valves market consists of various types, primarily including direct acting and pilot operated pressure relief valves. Direct acting pressure relief valves utilize a spring and disk mechanism to open and relieve pressure directly, providing simple and reliable operation. In contrast, pilot operated pressure relief valves use a pilot mechanism to control the opening of the main valve, offering better flow capacity and sensitivity. Other types may include rupture discs and combination valves, catering to specific applications and industries.</p></p>
