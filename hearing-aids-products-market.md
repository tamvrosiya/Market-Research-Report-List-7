<p><p>The hearing aids market is primarily categorized into In-the-ear (ITE) and Behind-the-ear (BTE) hearing aids. ITE hearing aids are custom-fitted to the user's ear canal, providing discretion and comfort, making them suitable for mild to moderate hearing loss. BTE hearing aids are worn behind the ear and connect to an earmold that sits inside the ear canal, offering greater amplification and flexibility for various hearing losses, making them ideal for a broader range of users and needs.</p></p>
