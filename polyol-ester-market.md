<p><ul><li>NPG</li><li>TMP</li><li>PE</li><li>DiPE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyol ester market encompasses various types primarily defined by their chemical structures and uses. NPG (Neopentyl Glycol) esters are known for excellent thermal stability and low volatility, ideal for lubricants. TMP (Trimethylolpropane) esters offer high biodegradability and are commonly used in food-grade applications. PE (Pentaerythritol) esters provide enhanced lubricity and low pour points, suitable for industrial use. DiPE (Dioctyl Pentaerythritol Ester) is employed for its performance in high-temperature environments, enhancing fluidity and stability in products.</p></p>
