<p><p>Brushless Synchronous Motors (BSMs) are advanced electric motors that use permanent magnets on the rotor and operate in sync with the alternating current (AC) supplied. BSMs are widely used in applications ranging from industrial automation and electric vehicles to HVAC systems and robotics.</p><p>The Brushless Synchronous Motors Market is poised for significant growth, anticipated to reach a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 8% during the forecast period.
